Ventures and Start-up Ideas

Innovative solutions and forward-thinking ideas by Valerie Fabbri Menna.

Current Ideas

Swift Endometriosis Diagnosis: Developing methods for faster, non-invasive diagnosis of endometriosis to improve early detection and treatment.

Assisting Mitral Valve Prolapse Patients: Creating devices or therapies to aid patients with mitral valve prolapse, enhancing quality of life and care.

Painless Dialysis Process: Innovating a painless, comfortable dialysis method for patients with kidney disease.

Delayed Drug Therapy for Cancer Patients: Designing a system for timed release of chemotherapy drugs through ports for more efficient treatment.

Vital Sign Tracking App: Developing an app that continuously monitors and records vital signs, providing a baseline for doctors in emergencies or when medical records are unavailable.

Emergency Water Safety Device: Developing a device that continuously monitors and records water quality through filter, providing a knowledgable report for health purposes.
