About Me

Hello! I'm Valerie, a student, creator, and dog mom! Combining my passions for chemical & materials engineering and microbiology, my ultimate career goal is to become a cardiovascular engineer. But I’m also a musician, a writer of a thrilling Sci-fi novel, and the Co-Founder of the MindSET app.

Portfolio

My work spans across multiple fields. From scientific research to creative projects, my portfolio reflects my love for blending art and science. Here are some key highlights:

  • MindSET App: A planning-focused mobile app designed to promote self-awareness and mental well-being.
  • Sci-Fi Novel: I'm in the process of writing an intriguing and illuminating novel that explores traditional art with a deep focus on human nature and technological advancements.
  • Music: I write and produce music, merging experimental sounds with electronic and atmospheric elements. April 18, 2025, I am releasing my first EP "Desolina."
